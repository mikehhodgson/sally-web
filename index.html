About Me

About Me

Bachelor of Health Science in Nutritional Medicine

Based in Perth Western Australia, I grew up heavily involved in a dancing school. It provided me with great life skills of discipline and reliability.

My interest with nutrition developed as a teenager and noticed the great effect it had on my own struggles with anxiety in my late teens.

The Dancer

Success as a dancer requires dedication, passion and many years of training. For a dancer, the body is the instrument and must be cared for. Optimal nutrition is essential to a dancer’s life, impacting their energy, strength and recovery. Nutrition education is one of the most important tools that a dancer can learn at any stage of their training or career.

Education is key
